Prospect House at 36 Prospect Street was acquired in 1920. Under the name of “ldquo;Ely House,”rdquo; the building provided both classroom and dormitory space. At one time it was known as “ldquo;Admiral Inn,”rdquo; so named by its residents, who devised the name in connection with their janitor Arthur Sims who bore the same last name as a well-known admiral. The building is now the home of the Department of Political Science.
